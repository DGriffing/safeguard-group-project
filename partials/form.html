Want To Join Our Study?

What would participating look like?

As a participant with 'Patients as Safeguards', you would be involved in Phase One, either an interview or survey. For Phase Two, six lucky participants will be chosen at random to participate in a design and prototyping phase for our newly developed technology. Lastly, Phase Three, twenty participants will be picked to test our final prototype, and offer their observations in an interview.

Reminder:

  • Participants must have been a patient/caregiver who had an overnight stay in a hospital within the past two years.
  • Caregiver is anyone who was closely involved with the patient at the time of the hospital stay, eg: Parent, Spouse, Child, Friend.
  • Patients must be 7 years of age or older.

Feedback from our participants is crutial for developing an application that will cut down on patient harm, and thus is invaluable. We thank you for taking the time to fill out this form, signing up for Patients as Safeguards.
